Launch Readiness Notes

Use this guide before launching a new website, campaign, or digital product update.

1. Core message
- Is the main offer final and approved?
- Are headlines, CTA labels, and service descriptions aligned?
- Has key copy been reviewed for clarity and consistency?

2. Visual checks
- Are final images, mockups, and brand assets optimized?
- Do layouts hold up on mobile, tablet, and desktop?
- Are hover states, forms, and interactive elements polished?

3. Conversion setup
- Are inquiry forms working?
- Is booking configured and tested?
- Are lead destinations, thank-you states, or follow-up steps clear?

4. SEO and discovery
- Are page titles and descriptions written?
- Is the sitemap included?
- Are important pages indexable?

5. Analytics and tracking
- Are CTA clicks and bookings being tracked?
- Are campaign links tagged correctly?
- Is there a clear plan for measuring results after launch?

6. Operational readiness
- Who owns updates after launch?
- What is the response plan if an issue appears?
- Is there a support or optimization window scheduled?

7. Final sign-off
- Has the business owner or lead stakeholder reviewed the live-ready version?
- Are all priority pages complete?
- Does the experience feel trustworthy and intentional?

Launches perform better when the message, visuals, and conversion path are aligned.
